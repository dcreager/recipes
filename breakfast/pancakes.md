# Pancakes

## Ingredients

- 1½ cup flour
- 3½ tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1¼ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp butter, melted

## Directions

1. Sift together dry ingredients.
2. Make well in center and pour in milk, egg, and melted butter; mix until
   smooth.
3. Cook some pancakes! (~⅓C batter per pancake)
