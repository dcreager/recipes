# Belgian waffles

Makes 5-6 waffles using our flippy Belgian waffle maker.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 4 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 2 cups milk

## Directions

1. Sift the dry ingredients together
2. Separate the eggs
3. Mix together egg yolks, milk, and oil
4. Add dry ingredients and mix
5. Beat egg whites and fold in
6. Cook some waffles
